help Is VOLTEIQ a normal stabilizer?

expand_more
VOLTEIQ is a Centralised Intelligent Voltage Protection system, not a basic stabilizer. It protects the entire installation and is engineered for modern grid conditions, handling fluctuations more precisely and faster than traditional mechanical stabilizers.

security Does it protect against high voltage and low voltage?

expand_more
Yes, VOLTEIQ monitors the incoming supply continuously. If the voltage exceeds or drops below safe thresholds, it instantly disconnects the load to prevent damage and restores power automatically once the supply stabilizes within safe limits.

analytics What is the 'Intelligent' part of VOLTEIQ?

expand_more
The intelligence lies in its microprocessor-controlled sensing logic. Unlike simple cut-off relays, VOLTEIQ analyzes the quality of power and incorporates smart delay timers to prevent frequent on/off switching, which is common during brownouts and can damage compressor-based appliances.

bolt How does it handle surges?

expand_more
VOLTEIQ is equipped with industrial-grade Surge Protection Devices (SPD) that clamp transient high-voltage spikes caused by lightning or grid switching, diverting excess energy safely to the ground.
info

Is VOLTEIQ required if an MCB or ELCB is already installed?

Yes. This is a common misconception. MCBs (Miniature Circuit Breakers) and ELCBs/RCCBs are designed for Current-based protection (overload, short circuits, and earth leakage).

  • MCB: Protects wires from melting due to too much current. It does not detect high voltage.
  • ELCB/RCCB: Protects people from electric shocks. It does not protect appliances from voltage fluctuations.
  • VOLTEIQ: Specifically handles Voltage-based protection. It fills the critical gap by protecting sensitive electronics from surges, sags, and swells that MCBs and ELCBs completely ignore.
