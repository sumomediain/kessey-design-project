About
Kessey Technologies
Pvt. Ltd.

KESSEY Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian electronics manufacturing and technology company specialising in intelligent electrical solutions for modern homes, buildings, and infrastructure.

Founded with a strong engineering foundation, Kessey focuses on the design, development, manufacturing, and deployment of electrical and electronic products that address critical challenges such as voltage instability, grid fluctuation, and power safety.

Operating as a multi-brand organisation, Kessey develops focused product brands, each built to solve a specific electrical problem with depth and clarity.

This structure allows us to innovate faster, scale responsibly, and maintain strong product integrity.

OUR VISION

To become a globally respected electrical technology company, known for setting new standards in intelligent voltage management, electrical safety, and future-ready power solutions across homes, buildings, and infrastructure.
