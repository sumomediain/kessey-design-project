Commonly Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about VOLTEIQ range of intelligent voltage protection and regulation products.
help Is VOLTEIQ a normal stabilizer? expand_more
VOLTEIQ is a Centralized Intelligent Voltage Protection system, not a basic stabilizer. It protects the entire installation and is engineered for modern grid conditions, handling fluctuations more precisely and faster than traditional mechanical stabilizers.
security Does it protect against high voltage and low voltage? expand_more
Yes, VOLTEIQ provides full-range protection, automatically correcting both high and low voltage anomalies to maintain a stable 230V output.
memory What is the 'intelligent' part of VOLTEIQ? expand_more
The intelligence lies in our proprietary CPU logic that predicts grid spikes and uses zero-cross switching to prevent electrical arcing during regulation.
bolt How does it handle surges? expand_more
It features a multi-stage surge suppression system that absorbs transient spikes and disconnects the load within milliseconds if the surge exceeds safe levels.

Is VOLTEIQ required if an MCB or ELCB is already installed?

Yes. This is a common misconception. MCBs (Miniature Circuit Breakers) and ELCBs/RCCBs are designed for Current-based protection (overload, short circuits, and earth leakage).

  • MCB: Protects wires from melting due to too much current. It does not detect high voltage.
  • ELCB/RCCB: Protects people from electric shocks. It does not protect appliances from voltage fluctuations.
  • VOLTEIQ: Specifically handles Voltage-based protection. It fills the critical gap by protecting sensitive electronics from surges, sags, and swells that MCBs and ELCBs completely ignore.
